About

 

Are you ready to dive into a world of innovation, creativity, and problem-solving? Welcome to HackMore, the ultimate hackathon where your ideas come to life and your skills shine! HackMore is not just another hackathon—it's a celebration of ingenuity and collaboration. Participants would work non-stop for 24 hours to prototype solutions for specific challenges or create new applications, products, or services. The Hackathon fosters creativity, teamwork, and rapid problem-solving under pressure, encouraging participants to push boundaries and explore innovative ideas. At the end of the event, teams would present their creations to judges or a panel, competing for prizes and recognition based on criteria such as creativity, technical accomplishment, novelty and feasibility. Beyond competition, would gather valuable learning experiences, networking opportunities, and a platform for launching new ventures or initiatives.

 

In addition to winning the prizes the participating teams are benefitted from HACKMoR through: 

  1. Networking:

    Connects with peers and mentors. Builds relationships for future collaborations or job opportunities.

  2. Funding for Projects:

    Opportunities to win grants or scholarships, and access to resources and potential investors for further project development.

  3. Patenting:

    Gain insights into intellectual property rights and patenting processes. Potential to develop patentable ideas and innovations.

  4. Pre-incubation Facility:

    Receive feedback and guidance from experienced mentors and judges. Access to pre-incubation programs and resources for early-stage startups.

  5. Collaboration and Teamwork:

    Experiences working in a diverse team. Learns task division, conflict management, and leveraging team strengths.

Manav Rachna University

 

Manav Rachna University (MRU)is a leading State Private University (established by Haryana State Legislature Act No 26 of 2014 & under section 2(f) of UGC Act 1956), offering globally relevant education. The University has evolved from Manav Rachna College of Engineering (MRCE), which was established in the year 2004, a NAAC accredited ‘A’ Grade institution. Manav Rachna University is among the Top 2 Emerging Engineering Institutions in India and has been ranked the No. 1 Engineering Institution in India for Research Capability & Placements in the Times Engineering Survey 2021. The accreditations/rankings are testimonials to the trust of accrediting bodies in the quality of education being offered, a well-established teaching and learning process guided by global best practices, and a culture of academic excellence promoting research, innovation & entrepreneurship.

 

About School of Engineering

 

The School of Engineering (SOE) is a division within a university that focuses on providing education, research, and training in various fields of Engineering and Technology. The school offers a wide range of programs, degrees, and resources to prepare students for careers in Engineering, Technology, and related industries. SOE at Manav Rachna University offers a variety of Undergraduate, Graduate, and Doctoral programs in various Engineering disciplines. These disciplines include Computer Science & Technology, Mechanical Engineering, and Electronics and Communication Engineering. SOE emphasis on Research and Innovation. Research within the school covers a wide range of topics, from developing new materials to designing advanced technologies. SOE has well-equipped laboratories and facilities where students can engage in experiments, design projects, and prototype development.

Challenge Areas (Choose any one)

  • C3. Blockchain for Transparency: Utilize blockchain technology to enhance transparency and security in financial transactions or supply chain finance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Hackmor'24 Hackathon?

The HackMoR'24 Hackathon is a 24-hour coding competition where teams of developers come together to build innovative projects. Participants will work on various themes, present their ideas, and compete for prizes.

Who can participate?

Students can participate in the team of size 1 - 4 in the hackathon. While a passion for technology and innovation is appreciated, no prior experience is necessary. Students pursuing any Degree/Diploma course in any University/College, can participate in this event.

How do I register?

Filling out the registration form on our website will allow you to participate in the HACKMoR Hackathon.

What is the format of the hackathon?

The HACKMoR Hackathon consists of two phases:
Phase 1: Online Mood,
Phase 2: Offline Mood.

What are the themes for this year?

The themes for HACKMoR Hackathon 2024 are:
- Healthcare
- Smart Cities
- Fintech
- Environment & Sustainability
- Open Innovations.

What are the prizes?

For the top three teams, there will be exciting awards worth of lakhs.

How will the projects be judged?

Projects will be judged based on the following criteria:
- Innovation: Creativity and originality of the idea.
- Impact: Potential impact and usefulness of the project.
- Technical Execution: Quality of the implementation and code.
- Presentation: Clarity and effectiveness of the project demo.

What happens if I face technical difficulties?

Please use the dedicated Discord channel to contact our support staff if you experience any technical difficulties. Throughout the event, technical support will be provided by us.

Who can I contact for more information?

Please use the assistance channel on our communication platform or email us at [support@hackmor.com](mailto:support@hackmor.com) with any questions or issues.

Are there any rules for the hackathon?

Reffer to our general guideline.
